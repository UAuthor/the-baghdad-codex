Description

This website, The Golden Library, has been created as a humble effort to gather and present biographies that are often difficult to access or scattered across the books of the scholars.
Its purpose is to serve as a clear and organized library — a codex — of the lives of the people of importance mentioned in the Qur’an and the Sunnah, as well as the companions, the scholars,
and other figures of significance in Islamic history.

The intention behind this work is to make knowledge more accessible and to assist Muslims in learning about the righteous predecessors, their lives, their struggles, and their contributions,
without difficulty or confusion. The biographies presented here are extracted and compiled from the works of the scholars, and the sources will be provided in every entry.

This project is a personal effort and has not been authenticated or reviewed by any scholar. It is undertaken while seeking knowledge and striving for clarity and correctness,
in the hope that it may benefit the Ummah and encourage learning about the companions and other important figures of Islam.
